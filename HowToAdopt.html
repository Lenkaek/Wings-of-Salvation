How to Adopt a Pet?

A Guide to Responsible Adoption

Preparation for Adoption

  • Assess your readiness to care for an animal
  • Prepare a place at home for your new family member
  • Acquire necessary supplies
  • Learn about pet care

Documents for Adoption

  • Passport
  • Proof of residence
  • Consent from all adult family members

First Steps After Adoption

  • Veterinary check-up
  • Quarantine and adaptation
  • Establishing contact
  • Regular check-ups and vaccinations
