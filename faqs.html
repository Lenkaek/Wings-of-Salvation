Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of the shelter? +

Our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need and to find loving homes for them. We strive to educate the community on responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

How can I adopt a pet? +

To adopt a pet, visit our shelter or check out our online gallery. Choose a pet and fill out the adoption form. Our team will guide you through the process.

What are your operating hours? +

We are open from 10 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Saturday. We are closed on Sundays.

Can I volunteer at the shelter? +

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Please contact us through our website or visit the shelter to learn more about volunteering opportunities.

How can I donate to the shelter? +

You can donate through our website, by bank transfer, or in person at the shelter. Your contributions help us provide food, medical care, and shelter for animals in need.
