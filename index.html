Hello, we are Wings of Salvation

Here we take care of homeless, lost and abandoned pets, providing them with warmth and protection. Our mission is to help every animal find a loving family and a new home. We create comfortable living conditions for our wards, providing them with everything they need: food, medical care and attention. Every day we work to give a chance for a happy life to those who deserve it. Join us in this noble cause! In Wings of Salvation, you can not only find a loyal friend, but also become part of a team that changes the lives of animals for the better.
